League Rules

  1. Period Length
    1. 3:00 warm-up
    2. 23:00 first half run time
    3. :30 Intermission
    4. 22:00 second half run time (last three minutes are stop time if game is within three goals
  2. Gameplay:
    1. Four on four
    2. No offsides
    3. No two line passes
    4. No icing
    5. If a game is tied at the end of a regular season game, the game ends in a tie.
    6. A win is worth two points
    7. A tie is worth 1 point
    8. A loss is worth no points
  3. Penalties
    1. All penalties are 3:00 run time
    2. Major penalties are an automatic ejection
    3. Fights are an automatic ejection and suspension (league discretion)
  4. Team Standings (Season)
    1. Team standings are kept throughout all 11 regular season games
    2. A tie in the standings (points) are determined in order by the following:
      1. Most wins
      2. Head to head record
      3. GF/GA Differential
      4. Lower team penalty minutes
      5. Coin flip
  5. Player Stats
    1. Stats are kept from the third week until the final game of the season (13 games in total)
  6. Playoffs
    1. Playoffs are a three game round robin format. The best regular season team record plays the weakest regular season team record, the second best regular season record team plays the second weakest regular season record team, etc.
    2. The top two seeds in the playoff round robin will play for the A championship. The third and fourth seeds in the playoff round robin will play for the B championship, etc.
    3. A tie in the playoff round robin points is determined by the following in order:
      1. Most playoff round robin wins
      2. Playoff head to head record
      3. Playoff GF/GA differential
      4. Playoff lower team penalty minutes
      5. Coin flip
    4. If a playoff round robin game is tied at the end of regulation, the game ends in a tie
    5. If a playoff championship game is tied at the end of regulation, there will be a 5:00 run-time sudden death period. If there is still a tie after the 5:00 sudden death period, there will be a shootout. The shootout will consist of three shooters for each team.   7. Miscellaneous rules
    1. Face-offs are dropped in each end or at centre (a five dot system)
    2. Games with over a six goal differential will be capped at a six goal differential. As an example, a 12-1 game will be logged as a 7-1 final. However, all points scored on the 12 goals will count towards individual player stats.
