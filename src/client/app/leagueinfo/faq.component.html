FAQs

How old do I have to be to play?

The two divisions we currently have will accommodate anyone 14 years of age and older.

What skill level are the leagues?

Our Senior division is our 18+ division that contains players that played house league through rep ice hockey growing up. It is considered a faster pace out of the two divisions.

Our Masters Double-X division is competitive but a slower pace than our Senior division. The Masters Double-X division contains players from the age of 14 to over 50, both men and women. If you are new to hockey or roller hockey, this would be a great division to try. This division is also a great division for fathers to play with their sons and/or daughters.

What equipment is required?

The only difference from ice hockey is the roller blades. Some of the more experienced players have switched over to a girdle and roller hockey pant in place of the standard hockey pant.

Can I sign-up as an individual?

Absolutely. East End Saints prides itself on competitive balance. Players are moved every season to ensure league parody.

Can I request to play with my friends?

Yes. However, depending on the caliber of the players, no guarantees can be made that they will remain on the same team throughout the whole season. Competitive balance supersedes player requests.

Can I enter a full team?

Yes. However, if the caliber of the team is not in line with the other teams in the division, players will need to be added or removed to ensure each team is competitive. There is no guarantee that a full team that is entered will remain together for the whole season.

Do goalies have to wear roller blades?

No. Goalies can either wear roller blades or shoes.
