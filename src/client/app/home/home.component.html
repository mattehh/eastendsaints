We offer divisions for almost every age group, all genders and all skill levels. So come out and join us, we always have room for more players.

About the League

Founded in 1995, the East End Saints Roller Hockey League offers a place to play for individuals of all genders and almost all ages for 15 weeks every summer. With 2 divisions, a large concrete playing surface, and great group of players we aim to provide the best league around! So what are you waiting for? Sign up below!

Register Now!

The league operates out of the Ajax Community Center from the last week of April and to the last week of July (15 games) for only 225$.

Complete the registration form to get a spot in the league for this season.

Double-X provides a more relaxed environment including a place for families to play together and have some fun on Tuesday nights
